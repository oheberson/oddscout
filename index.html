Privacy Policy for Odd Scout

Last Updated: September 2025

Overview

Odd Scout is a Chrome extension designed to help users filter betting odds on supported websites. This privacy policy explains how we handle data in relation to this extension.

Data Collection and Usage

No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The extension operates entirely within your browser and does not communicate with external servers.

Local Data Storage

The extension stores the following data locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API:

  • Odds Range Settings: Your minimum and maximum odds preferences
  • Extension State: Whether the filter is currently active

This data remains on your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Website Access

The extension accesses betting odds data displayed on Betano.bet.br pages to:

  • Scan the page for betting odds elements
  • Filter and highlight odds within your specified range
  • Hide or dim odds outside your range

This processing occurs entirely within your browser and does not involve sending data to external servers.

Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions:

  • activeTab: To access the current tab and inject filtering functionality
  • tabs: To identify supported betting websites
  • scripting: To inject content scripts that scan and filter odds
  • Host permissions (betano.bet.br): To access betting odds content on Betano's website
  • storage: To save your preferences locally in your browser

Third-Party Services

This extension does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks.

Data Security

Since all data processing occurs locally in your browser and no data is transmitted externally, your information remains secure on your device.

Children's Privacy

This extension is not intended for use by anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or the Odd Scout extension, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store listing or GitHub repository.
